I have been involved in many projects, you will find below a small description of some of them


ECG in MRI


My first and main project is the processing of ECG signals acquired in a MRI environment. The ECG is corrupted by different artefacts induced by the magnetic field gradients or by the MagnetoHydrodynamic effect, which is induced by the blood flow in a large magnetic field.

mHealth


We have developed an innovative end-to-end system allowing us to acquire ECG signal on a mobile, analyse the signal, upload the data on a Medical record system, where clinicians can have access to it for further analysis, where annotations from multiple clinicians can be aggregated.


back on top


Foetal ECG


I have been heavily involved on a foetal ECG project. We proposed accurate and innovative techniques for the extraction of foetal R peaks from maternal abdominal recordings. We have also developed a realistic model of foetal ECG recordings. Finally, we have also proposed new techniques for the analysis of the FECG morphology, especially QT measurement.
